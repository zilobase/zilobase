# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We pledge to make participation in Notelab a respectful and harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and expression, level of experience, education, socioeconomic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, caste, color, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intent, but take responsibility for your impact.
- Give and receive feedback professionally.
- Keep discussions focused on the project and the people using it.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, intimidation, stalking, or sustained disruption.
- Insults, slurs, sexualized language, or unwelcome sexual attention.
- Publishing private information without explicit permission.
- Any conduct that would reasonably make contributors feel unsafe or unwelcome.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove comments, close issues, reject contributions, or temporarily or permanently ban participants who violate this Code of Conduct.

Report conduct concerns privately to `hey@sreeragh.me`. Maintainers will review reports as promptly as possible and handle them with appropriate confidentiality.
